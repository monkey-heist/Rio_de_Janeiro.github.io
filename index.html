About Rio De Janeiro

Rio De Janeiro is a unique video game where monkeys face off in exciting 1vs1 battles. Mixing strategy, action and humour, this game immerses you in an intense universe where every decision counts. Get ready for realistic graphics and captivating gameplay!

Breathtaking visuals that immerse you in the world of the arena.

Choice of weapons

Customise your strategy with a vast choice of unique weapons, adapted to each style of play.

The story of Rio De Janeiro

In a world where jungles can no longer satisfy the monkeys' thirst for glory and riches, a new tradition was born: the Heist Arena. Here, the cleverest, fastest, and boldest monkeys face off in epic matches for supremacy. The ultimate prize? The legendary Golden Banana, a symbol of power and domination.

The battles take place in a one-of-a-kind arena, a monumental stadium built in the heart of the jungle, where thousands of roaring spectators cheer on their champions. Each match pits two monkeys against each other, dressed in impeccable suits reminiscent of legendary movie heisters. These monkeys must infiltrate their opponent's base to steal their Golden Banana while defending their own.

A Duel of Ruse and Strategy

In Monkey Heist, it’s not just about brute strength. Each duel is a complex dance of strategy, clever traps, and heart-pounding chases. Monkeys can use their environment and even distract their opponent to infiltrate their vault.

The Rival Clans

  • The Banana Shadows: Stealth experts.
  • The Jungle Kings: Aggressive heisters with brutal strategies.

Each victory in the arena strengthens their clan’s reputation, allowing its members to achieve ultimate glory.

Why steal the Golden Banana?

The Golden Banana is more than just a trophy. It is a mystical relic that, according to legend, grants superior intelligence and eternal dominance over other monkeys. For the clans, possessing it represents more than just a victory: it is a symbol of absolute power.

The public: The True Kings

The matches are broadcast throughout the jungle via ultra-fast messenger snails. Bets are placed, alliances are formed, and monkeys from all corners of the world dream of seeing their champion prevail.

And you?

Put on your suit, plan your heist, and get ready to enter the arena. In Monkey Heist, glory belongs to those who dare to risk it all. But beware: one wrong move, and your Golden Banana will be gone.

Project timeline

  • Start

    The project began with an in-depth brainstorming phase in September 2024. This stage established the conceptual foundations of ‘Rio De Janeiro’ and explored ideas for gameplay, game mechanics and visual identity. These considerations led to a clear definition of the project's objectives and the identification of the tools needed to achieve them. At the same time, functional and technical specifications were drawn up to define the expected functionalities and specify the technologies to be used and the development stages.

  • Submission n°1

    In January 2025, the first presentation was given of the first prototypes and the elements that had already been modelled. This presentation offered an overview of the work carried out, highlighting the basic gameplay mechanisms, modelling and the first technical implementations. It also provided an opportunity to obtain feedback to guide the next phases.

  • Submission n°2

    Planned for March 2025, the second presentation will focus on the results of the tests, the improvements made to the gameplay and the final additions, such as music, sound effects and the full integration of user interfaces. The feedback received during this presentation has enabled us to refine the details of the project.

  • Final submission

    In May 2025, the final presentation will mark the completion of the project. The complete game, including an optimised website, official trailer and gameplay, will be finalised. This will be an opportunity to highlight all the aspects of the game and to consider the release or launch phase.
